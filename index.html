    • What is Treaty 1?

    Treaty 1 was a treaty made in Lower Fort Garry and established Western Canada. It was the first treaty ever made with 6 more being made to control the land, and stop american invasion. The treaty is to be in place for as long as the sun shines, grass grows, and water flows.

    Who is Apart of Treaty 1?

    There are 7 nations who are apart of treaty 1. Peguis First Nation, Sagkeeng First Nation, Brokenhead Ojibway Nation, Roseau River Anishinaabe First Nation, Long Plain First Nation, Sandy Bay Ojibway First Nation, Swan Lake First Nation. Every nation is represented on the Treaty 1 Flag
